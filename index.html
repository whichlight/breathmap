About

Up in the Air lets you Journey across the world as the wind. The path represents the larger-scale wind trajectory drawing from over 30 years of atmospheric data*. Blow into the microphone to start your adventure.


There will be a few stops along the way. You'll see events from the past 30 years at each stop. Keep blowing, you're the wind!


*Climatological long-term monthly means, thanks to Unidata IDV and NCEP Reanalysis data provided by the NOAA/OAR/ESRL PSD, Boulder, Colorado, USA.
